woocosms

Skipping past the other names of AMPMCraft1523, Lord Pikablu, and Pikablu127, I think I've found a home with the name woocosms. I often tend to iterate between keeping or adding the "s" at the end, and whether or not I want to stick to Paracosms or not.

The name originally comes from the Geometry Dash demonlist level Hyper Paracosm, which for a while, used to be my Minecraft username. One day my friend suggested the switch to just Paracosm, to have a more recognizable name. Unfortunately, since it was taken, I stuck with HyperParacosm. Since Paracosm was taken, my friend suggested to just switch to Paracosms instead. This is my go-to name for most online areas, and where the "cosms" comes from in woocosms.

Unfortunately still, Paracosms is not unique enough to be able to be available on every website. This led me to iterate on my username again. As for where this comes from, sometimes when my friends texted my name they'd write "andrewew", similarly sounding to andrewoo. The "woo" in woocosms comes from my Discord nickanme in a server of my friends, where my nickname there is simply "woo".
